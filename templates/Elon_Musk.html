Elon Musk

Tesla, SpaceX

Net Worth: $11.1 billion

Timeline

1971: Born

For his bachelor's degree, he first attends Queen's University in Ontario, Canada, but ends up transferring to Penn University to study economics and physics.

He then starts his Ph.D. at Stanford, but drops out shortly after to focus on his entrepreneurial pursuits.

1995: With his brother, Musk co-founds Zip2 Corporation, an online city guide. Here's the story of his first company:

1999: Sells Zip2 Corporation to Compaq Computers for $307 million in cash and $34 million in stock options.

2000: X.com, a company founded by Musk, acquires Confinity, of which Peter Thiel was a co-founder, and becomes Paypal. When this happened, he owned 11% of the company's stock.

2002: Founds SpaceX.

2002: Sells Paypal to eBay for $1.5 billion.

2004: Musk leads the $7.5 million financing round for Tesla and becomes the chairman of the board.

2016: Tesla acquires SolarCity, a full-service solar provider of which Musk is the chairman.

Present: He currently serves as the CEO of Tesla, and the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX.
